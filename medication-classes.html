Medication Class Interaction Guides

High-yield MedCheck Engine starting points for common medication groups and known interaction patterns: pharmacogenomics, CYP inhibition or induction, active metabolites, transporters, bleeding, QT, electrolytes, acid suppression, and PK exposure.

Scale-first grouping. These guides prioritize classes many people actually use, not only rare high-severity specialist drugs. The first three links are quick-start examples; each card then opens a longer set of known examples.

Scale lens: CDC/NCHS reports broad prescription-drug and office-visit drug-therapy use, with analgesics and antihyperlipidemic agents among frequent therapeutic classes; ClinCalc DrugStats adds recent MEPS-derived signals such as statin, GLP-1/GIP, SGLT2, and anticoagulant utilization.

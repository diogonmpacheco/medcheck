Not a clinical ranking. These are discovery prompts for common-use or high-review-burden classes. All MedCheck evidence remains source-linked and pending professional review.

Scale rationale uses public U.S. prescription-use signals from CDC/NCHS and MEPS-derived ClinCalc DrugStats; examples are chosen because they are useful MedCheck discovery prompts, not because they are the most dangerous combinations.
